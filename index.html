About Vieli FC

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Vieli FC has grown from a local community team to a competitive force in regional football. Our club is built on values of teamwork, respect, and excellence.

With state-of-the-art training facilities and a dedicated coaching staff, we nurture talent at all levels, from youth development to our professional squad.

Our home ground, the Vieli Arena, is known for its electric atmosphere and passionate supporters who create an unforgettable matchday experience.

We're proud to be sponsored by KUDA, sharing our commitment to innovation and performance.

New Youth Academy Launched

Community Program Reaches 10,000 Kids

Vieli FC's community outreach program has now engaged with over 10,000 local children through free coaching sessions and school visits.
